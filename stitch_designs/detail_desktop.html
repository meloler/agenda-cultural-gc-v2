About the Event

Join us for a magical evening where classical mastery meets the rhythmic sounds of the ocean. The Gran Canaria Philharmonic Orchestra returns to the iconic Alfredo Kraus Auditorium for their annual open-air gala. This year's program features a curated selection of romantic era masterpieces designed to evoke the spirit of the sea and the stars.

The evening will commence with Beethoven's "Pastoral Symphony," followed by a brief intermission with complimentary local wines. The second half crescendoes into Tchaikovsky's 1812 Overture, complete with a synchronized light show that illuminates the coastline.
